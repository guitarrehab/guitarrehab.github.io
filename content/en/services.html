In our workshop we offer instrument setup and tuning, repair, restoration and customization services, for both electric and acoustic instruments.

We provide the following services:

  • Basic setup
  • Setup with fret dressing
  • Refretting
  • Handcrafted bone nuts and bridge saddles
  • Shielding of electronic components
  • Pickup replacement
  • Pre-amplifier installation on acoustic guitars
  • Truss rod repair
  • Structural repairs like broken headstocks, damaged soundboards or unglued bridges, among others
  • Painting and finishing
  • Instrument customization

Terms and Conditions

The value for services depend on the specific condition of each instrument and/or equipment. We suggest that you schedule a visit the workshop so we can evaluate your case and present you a detailed budget. Visits by previous appointment.

Modifications and customizarion requests are subject to analysis.

After contact to inform that the work is done, we have a 10 work days period for payment and instrument(s) pickup. After that period, and with no contact of resonable justification for delay, a daily storage fee will be applied over the instrument(s) until retrieval.

For further information about our services, please contact us at guitarrehablisbon@gmail.com .
